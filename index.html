Daily routine

Built for the habit, not the hack.

Most gut routines die in the first month because they ask too much of you. Powders to mix. Capsules to swallow on an empty stomach. Drinks that taste like a punishment. Pheeew is one pouch, one sip, room temperature, anywhere — your fridge, your bag, your desk drawer. The pouch is the point.

We're not asking you to overhaul your diet. We're asking you to give your gut 30 seconds, every day, for the next eight weeks. The fiber and probiotic compound; the routine stops being a thought. By day 30 your gut starts running on autopilot. That's the whole product.

When to drink it

Anytime, but mornings are the magic.

A pouch with breakfast. Or with coffee. Or right before lunch. There's no wrong time — but a consistent time builds the habit faster.

Cold tastes like a slushy. Frozen tastes like a popsicle. Room temp tastes like a smoothie you forgot in your car. All three work for your gut.

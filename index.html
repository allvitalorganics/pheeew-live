  2. ~16g average intake. CDC NHANES 2017–2018 dietary survey, mean US adult fiber intake. CDC NHANES. FDA Daily Value 28g per 21 CFR 101.9.

  4. Probiotic gastric loss. Mainville I et al. Int J Food Microbiol. 2005 — PMID 16253381. Non-spore Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium commonly lose >99% of viability in simulated gastric conditions without protective delivery.

  7. Fibersol-2L evidence. Burns et al. 2018 — PMID 29390888; Ye et al. 2015 — PMID 26081511. Most-studied resistant maltodextrin for tolerance, regularity, and glucose response.
  8. XOS bifidogenic. Finegold et al. 2014 — PMID 24611617. Selective Bifidobacterium increase at 1.4–2.8 g/day.

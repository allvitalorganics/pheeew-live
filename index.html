Honest answers

What people ask.

Will it actually work for me?−

Most people notice less bloat in 3–7 days. Bowel rhythm benefits arrive around day 7–14. Microbiome shifts take 30+ days. Your body decides the pace. If it doesn't work, return it.

How fast does it kick in?−

Fastest signal is bloat reduction (3–7 days). Bowel rhythm follows (1–2 weeks). The microbiome takes longer (30+ days). Daily use is what makes it land.

Will I bloat at first?−

Sometimes — for 2–3 days, if your fiber baseline is low. Halve the dose for the first week if intense, then build up.

Why three ingredients, not more?−

Because every ingredient has to earn its weight. Three at full dose lands harder than five at half-dose.

Do I need to refrigerate it?−

No. The probiotic is spore-form — it stays stable at room temperature. Throw a stick in your bag.

Honest answers

What people ask.

Will it actually work?−

Most people notice less bloat in 3–7 days. Bowel rhythm improves around day 7–14. Microbiome shifts take 30+ days. If it doesn't work for you, return it within 60 days.

How fast does it kick in?−

Fastest signal: bloat down (3–7 days). Then bowel rhythm (1–2 weeks). Then microbiome (30+ days). Daily use is what makes it land.

Will I bloat at first?−

Sometimes — for 2–3 days, if your fiber baseline is low. Halve the dose for the first week if it's intense, then build up.

Why three ingredients, not eight?−

Three at full clinical dose lands harder than five at half-dose. Each ingredient has its own human-trial evidence — added together, they're a complementary synbiotic per the ISAPP 2020 definition.

Do I need to refrigerate it?−

No. The probiotic is spore-form — stable at room temperature. Toss a stick in your bag.

When will I feel something?
Most people feel less bloat by day 7. Predictable mornings by week 4. Give it 30 days for a real read. Soluble fiber needs 72 hours to start feeding the right bacteria; spore probiotics need 5–7 days to germinate.
Can I take it with coffee?
Yes. The probiotic is spore-form — survives coffee, stomach acid, and the journey to your gut intact. Take it whenever fits your day.
Is this safe during my period?
Yes — we made it specifically for the days your body locks up, period bloat included. Day 1 of cycle is the most common time founders take it daily.
GLP-1 friendly?
Yes. Specifically formulated for the gut side effects of GLP-1 medications (Ozempic, Wegovy, etc.). Many founders are on GLP-1s and find Pheeew helps with the digestive lock-up they cause.
What if it doesn't work for me?
30-day refund. No "tell us about your symptoms" form. No back-and-forth. Reply to any email from us with the word "refund" — Dawon (real human) processes it the same day.

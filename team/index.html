"Down the Hole."
The gut-talk podcast.

Long-form interviews with gastroenterologists, microbiome researchers, fermentation scientists, and founders who think about gut health for a living. Title nods to where the spore actually goes — and to the rabbit hole gut science is.

Why we're doing it: trust takes time and a single podcast appearance from a real expert outperforms ten polished ad creatives. The podcast is the credibility moat.

Cadence target: 1–2 episodes/month at launch. Each episode → 8–12 short clips Dawon distributes across IG/TikTok/X. Audio on Spotify, Apple, YouTube.

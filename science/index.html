Three studies. By name.

2022 · IBS

IBS efficacy RCT

N: 92 patients, IBS-C + IBS-D
Dose: 500M CFU twice daily
Duration: 60 days
Design: Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled
Result: 90% of IBS-C and 100% of IBS-D responded vs 0% placebo.

2021 · Constipation

Transit time RCT

N: Healthy adults with mild constipation
Duration: 8 weeks
Design: Randomized, controlled
Outcome: Transit time + bowel movement quality
Result: Significant transit-time improvement and bowel-discomfort reduction vs placebo.

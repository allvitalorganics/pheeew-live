◆ The Story

The brand started
when her gut gave up.

Pheeew exists because of one specific window in our co-founder's life — a stretch where she couldn't poop for two weeks at a time. The product is the answer she went looking for and couldn't find.

Chapter 1 · The break

She had chased looking right. Her body kept the receipts.

Dawon — our CBO and the face of Pheeew — spent her early 20s chasing a particular kind of body. She did all the things. Calorie counting. Diet pills she didn't fully understand from clinics that didn't fully explain them. Restriction so consistent it didn't feel like restriction. She got what she wanted.

And then her gut stopped.

Two-week stretches without going. Bloating that turned eating into a math problem. Skin that started reacting to food the way it used to react to anxiety. The body — the one she'd been trying to shape from the outside — had a different idea about who was in charge.

Chapter 2 · The search

She tried every gut product on every shelf.

Probiotics in capsules that needed refrigeration and an empty stomach. Powders that turned breakfast into a science experiment. "Wellness" drinks that tasted like punishment and cost $14 a bottle. The kombuchas, the shots, the gummies, the gut-healing protocols sold by influencers who'd never had a real gut problem.

The products that worked were too much work. The products that were easy didn't work. Everything in the gut aisle felt designed for someone who wanted to perform being healthy — not for someone who needed their body to stop running the show.

The product she needed didn't exist.

Chapter 3 · The build

Four of us. One pouch.

Pheeew is built by four people. Alex — our CEO — writes the brand and runs ops. Jason — COO — runs finance, legal, and supply. Justin — CGO — runs growth, partners, and the manufacturer relationship. Dawon — CBO — is the brand's voice, the face you'll see in every reel and carousel, the person whose gut started the whole thing.

We tested formats for a year. Capsule (too much like a supplement). Stick pack of powder (too much like a chore). Drink in a bottle (too expensive, too cold, too "look at me being healthy"). The pouch is the format that survived: 80mL, hot-fill, shelf-stable, one sip, anywhere.

The actives took longer. We landed on a synbiotic blend: 10g of prebiotic fiber (XOS + Fibersol-2) plus 1 billion CFU of a spore-form probiotic strain (Bacillus coagulans SNZ 1969) that's heat-stable, well-studied, and the same strain inside a handful of products the rest of the world quietly trusts.

The flavor — Mojave Sunrise — came last. Tropical fruit, mango, banana. Sweet enough to feel like a treat. Light enough to feel like food, not a dessert. Built for the morning ritual.

What we believe

Gut care is a habit, not a project.

Most wellness products win on aspirational use and lose on actual use. People buy them with their best self in mind, then quietly give up by week three. Pheeew is built for the people week three eats alive — which is most of us.

We design for what you'll actually finish. One pouch. One sip. Room temperature. No ritual to remember. No empty stomach to plan around. No bag of supplements to travel with. The morning takes thirty seconds. The compound effect takes a month. The brand is the eight-week version of you that didn't quit.

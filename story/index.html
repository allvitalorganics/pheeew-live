The build, in chapters

Eight months,
twelve formulas.

We deleted ingredients more often than we added them. The version we're shipping has four. Here's how it got there.

2025 · Q3

The complaint

Both of us bought gut products in two countries and didn't trust either of them. The American ones were bricks of stevia. The Korean ones were 3-gram fiber gummies wearing a 30-gram lab coat.

2025 · Q4

First conversation with Daoom

A GMP-certified Korean powder facility. We brought a brief: smallest possible formula, real fiber dose, named strain, no sweeteners.

2025 · Nov

Formula v1.0 — too ambitious

11 ingredients, 7 functional claims, multiple flavor variants. Looked great on a deck. Wouldn't have shipped.

2026 · Jan

v2.0 — starting to delete

Dropped flavor masking. Dropped two adaptogens we couldn't dose meaningfully in 5g. Down to 7 ingredients.

2026 · Feb

v3.0 — strain locked

Switched probiotic from a non-spore strain to B. coagulans SNZ 1969 (Sanzyme). Spore-form gives shelf stability without the cold chain.

2026 · Mar

v3.5 — went unflavored

The decision that changed the brand. Without flavoring, we could push the fiber dose to 14g — which actually crosses the MFDS thresholds for two functional claims.

2026 · Apr

v4.0 — locked

Four ingredients: 12g Fibersol-2L, 2g XOS, 2 billion CFU SNZ 1969, trace silica. Daoom R&D submitted. Full formula →

2026 · Q3

First sticks ship

Founders' list opens at $29 trial pricing for the first 200 customers. Then standard ladder.

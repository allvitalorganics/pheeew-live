The build, in chapters

Eight months. Twelve formulas.

We deleted ingredients more often than we added them. The version shipping has three core ingredients. Here's how it got there.

2025 · Q3

The complaint

Both of us bought gut products in two countries and didn't trust either. The American ones were dosed for marketing. The Korean ones were dosed for regulatory minimums. None of them were dosed for results.

2025 · Q4

First conversations

We brought a brief to manufacturers in Korea, India, and the US: smallest possible formula, real fiber dose, named strain, no sweeteners. Every conversation started with someone explaining why our brief wasn't possible.

2025 · Nov

Formula v1.0 — too ambitious

11 ingredients, 7 functional claims, multiple flavor variants. Looked great on a deck. Wouldn't have shipped a single SKU we believed in.

2026 · Jan

v2.0 — starting to delete

Dropped flavor masking. Dropped two adaptogens we couldn't dose meaningfully. Down to seven ingredients.

2026 · Feb

v3.0 — strain locked

Switched probiotic from a vegetative-form strain to Bacillus coagulans SNZ 1969 from Sanzyme. Spore-form gives shelf stability without the cold chain.

2026 · Mar

v3.5 — went unflavored, then re-flavored honestly

Stripped flavor entirely to test pure dose. Tasted like fiber. Re-introduced peach purée only — no stevia, no monk fruit, no sucralose. The peach carries the flavor on its own.

2026 · Apr

v4.0 — locked

Three core ingredients: 8g Fibersol-2L (ADM), 2g XOS, 1×10⁹ live spore cultures of B. coagulans SNZ 1969 (Sanzyme). Plus filtered water, peach purée, citric acid, natural peach flavor, ascorbic acid as preservative. R&D submitted.

2026 · Q3

First sticks ship

Founders' list opens at $29 trial pricing for the first 200 customers. Then standard ladder. The sub-vs-one-time math says subscribe, but we're not making one-time customers feel bad about being one-time customers.

The thesis

Lean portfolio.
Heavy lifters.

Pheeew is the first brand from Pheeew, Inc. — a Delaware C-Corp founded by Alex Kim, Jason Ko, and Dawon Kim. The plan isn't to build twenty brands. It's to build a small number of brands that each earn their place.

3

Anchor ingredients

Every Pheeew product starts with Fibersol-2L, XOS, and SNZ 1969. New formulations add function-specific actives — never to fix the foundation, only to extend it.

1

Hero SKU at launch

Gut + Bloat ships first. Future formulations (Gut + Steady, others) follow only when the human-trial evidence justifies a new claim. We don't ship fiction.

0

Filler ingredients

No stevia. No monk fruit. No sucralose. No proprietary blends hiding under-doses. If it's on the label, it's there because the literature says it does work.

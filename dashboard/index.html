The Brand

Positioning, voice, mission. What Pheeew is and isn't.
Storyline brief → Operating playbook →
Positioning
One paragraph the team agrees on

Pheeew is a smoothie for your gut. An 80mL synbiotic puree pouch — real fruit blended with 10g of prebiotic fiber and a clinically-studied probiotic strain — for people who tried doing more for their gut, gave up, and want something they'll actually finish. We're not a juice. We're not a shot. We're not a supplement. We're the food version of the gut routine.

Tagline (placeholder)
"Your gut knows."
Final direction: warmer Poppi/Olipop × Suja/Vive feel. Not locked.
Architecture
How we name what's next
Gut + (function).
Every SKU is "Gut + [a second thing your body actually feels]." Phase 1 anchor is Tropical Punch. Subsequent SKUs pace based on Phase 1 signal — paired with body benefits the audience can name in a sentence. No "wellness" buzzwords.
Phase 1 · Tropical Punch · 열대과일·망고·바나나
Phase 2 · Berry-forward · 블루베리·사과·딸기·크랜베리·엘더베리 · TBD order

Voice pillars
How the brand sounds when Dawon speaks for it
01 · Discovery, not teacher
"Here's what I tried" — never "here's what you should do."
Audience is brought along as a witness, not a student. The Glossier Into the Gloss side of the line, not the "10 products you need" side.
02 · Body, not aesthetic
The gut is a place to live in, not look at.
No before/after weight loss. No body language as the metric. The win is how you feel inside your skin, not how your skin looks.
03 · Feeling first, then mechanism
Hero copy speaks in feelings. Science lives on the science page.
"Synbiotic," "Bacillus coagulans SNZ 1969," "clinically-studied" — these are footer language and PDP technicals. The front door talks about how it feels, when you'd use it, why you'd come back.
04 · Earned, not announced
Pheeew shows up in Episode 11 — not Episode 1.
Dawon's first ten episodes are her own story. The brand is the answer she found, not the thing she's selling. Soft presence in captions (waitlist link only) until the story earns the right to name it.

The functional ingredients
Probiotic
Bacillus coagulans SNZ 1969®
Sanzyme Biologics, India. Spore-form, heat-stable to 180°F — enables hot-fill in the pouch. 30+ clinical studies including IBS, constipation, and immunomodulatory trials.
Prebiotic · 10g per pouch
XOS + Fibersol-2 blend
XOS (xylo-oligosaccharide) is a short-chain prebiotic fermented selectively by Bifidobacteria. Fibersol-2® (Matsutani/ADM) is a clinically-studied soluble resistant maltodextrin that adds bulk + supports regularity. Combined at 10g/pouch — roughly 30–40% of the FDA's daily fiber recommendation in a single 80mL serve.
Synbiotic = prebiotic + probiotic. Never "postbiotic" — that's a different category.

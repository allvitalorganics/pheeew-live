FAQ

The fast answers.

How does it taste?

Tropical fruit, mango, banana. Sweet from the fruit. No added sugar. Most people say smoothie-like at room temp; slushy when cold; popsicle when frozen.

When do I drink it?

Whenever. Consistency builds the habit faster than timing. Most people pick mornings because the routine sticks.

When do I feel something?

Week 1: gut wakes up. Week 2–3: morning rhythm settles. Week 4–6: the compound effect — downstream signals (skin, energy) sometimes follow. Week 8+: it's just routine.

What's actually in it?

Real fruit puree, 10g of prebiotic fiber, 1 billion CFU of probiotic, and a touch of lemon juice. That's the entire ingredient list. No water, no sweeteners, no flavoring, no coloring, no preservatives.

Allergens?

None of the top 9. Vegan, gluten-free, non-GMO. Allergen-free production line.

Does it need a fridge?

No. Shelf-stable 18 months at room temp. Refrigerate after opening (for taste, not safety). The cap reseals.

Cancel my subscription?

From your account. One click. No phone call.

Where does it ship?

Continental US, Alaska, Hawaii at launch. International is a Year 2+ question.

Honest answers

Common questions.

Probiotic or prebiotic?−

Both. The fiber feeds the bacteria. The spore is the bacteria. Together they're a synbiotic.

Why spore-form?−

Most probiotics die in stomach acid. Spore-form survives because it stays dormant until the small intestine signals the wake-up.

How long until I notice it?−

Most patterns show in 3 to 7 days for bloat. Regularity benefits arrive around day 7 to 14. Microbiome shifts take 30+ days.

Will I get bloated at first?−

Sometimes for 2–3 days, if your fiber baseline is low. Halve the dose for the first week if intense.

Why three? Why not more?−

Because every additional ingredient has to earn its weight. Three at full dose beats five at half-dose.

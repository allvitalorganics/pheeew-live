## Filing Instructions

1. Print, sign as CEO with wet ink (or Adobe Fill & Sign with valid digital signature)
2. Scan/save as PDF
3. File with Delaware Division of Corporations:
   - Path A: https://corp.delaware.gov/ portal upload
   - Path B (recommended): Stripe Atlas dashboard amendment workflow
4. Pay filing fee (~$200, payable by credit card)
5. Receive Stamped Filed Copy by email within 1-3 business days
6. Save Stamped Filed Copy as: `_DE_Filing/Pheeew_Certificate-of-Amendment_DE-Filed_2026-05-XX.pdf`

**DO NOT proceed to Phase 5 (RSPAs / share issuances) until the Stamped Filed Copy is received.** The new shares cannot be issued until the COI authorizes them.

1. Print the signed original plus one (1) copy.
2. Mail to the Internal Revenue Service Center for the state in which the Taxpayer will file his federal individual income tax return for taxable year 2026. For other states, look up the correct Service Center at irs.gov ("Where to file Form 1040").
3. Use **USPS Certified Mail with Return Receipt** (~$8 postage). Retain the green Certified Mail receipt and the green Return Receipt card when it arrives.

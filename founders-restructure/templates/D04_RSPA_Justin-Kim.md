# RESTRICTED STOCK PURCHASE AGREEMENT

## PHEEEW, INC.

*A Delaware Corporation*

---

This Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement (this "**Agreement**") is made and entered into as of **[DATE]** (the "**Effective Date**"), by and between **Pheeew, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation (the "**Company**"), and **Junhyuk Kim** ("**Purchaser**"), an individual.

---

## ARTICLE 1. PURCHASE AND SALE OF STOCK

1.1 **Purchase.** Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, on the Effective Date Purchaser hereby purchases from the Company, and the Company hereby issues and sells to Purchaser, Three Million Seventeen Thousand One Hundred Forty-Three (3,017,143) shares of the Company's Common Stock, par value $0.0001 per share (the "**Shares**"), at a purchase price of $0.00226 per share, for an aggregate purchase price of Six Thousand Eight Hundred Eighteen Dollars and Seventy-Four Cents ($6,818.74) (the "**Purchase Price**").

1.2 **Payment.** The Purchase Price shall be paid by Purchaser to the Company on or before the Effective Date by wire transfer of immediately available funds to the Company's designated bank account.

1.3 **Additional Capital Contribution.** In addition to the Purchase Price, Purchaser shall make a voluntary capital contribution to the Company in the amount of Twenty-Three Thousand One Hundred Eighty-One Dollars and Twenty-Six Cents ($23,181.26) (the "**Additional Capital Contribution**"), to be recorded by the Company as Additional Paid-in Capital. The Additional Capital Contribution is not consideration for the Shares, and no additional shares shall be issued to Purchaser in respect of the Additional Capital Contribution. The aggregate cash payment by Purchaser to the Company under this Agreement (Purchase Price plus Additional Capital Contribution) is Thirty Thousand Dollars ($30,000.00).

1.4 **Issuance.** Upon receipt of the Purchase Price, the Company shall issue Stock Certificate No. CS-004 to Purchaser representing the Shares.

---

## ARTICLE 2. VESTING

2.1 **Vesting Schedule.** The Shares shall vest as follows: twenty-five percent (25%) of the Shares shall vest on the one (1) year anniversary of the Effective Date (the "**Cliff Date**"), and the remaining seventy-five percent (75%) of the Shares shall vest in equal monthly installments over the following thirty-six (36) months, such that one hundred percent (100%) of the Shares shall be fully vested on the four (4) year anniversary of the Effective Date, subject to Purchaser's continuous service to the Company.

2.2 **Vesting Commencement Date.** The Vesting Commencement Date is the Effective Date.

2.3 **Termination of Service.** If Purchaser's continuous service to the Company terminates for any reason prior to the full vesting of the Shares, all unvested Shares shall be subject to the Repurchase Option set forth in Article 3.

---

## ARTICLE 3. REPURCHASE OPTION

3.1 **Repurchase Right.** The Company shall have the option (but not the obligation) to repurchase any or all unvested Shares (the "**Repurchase Option**") at the original Purchase Price of $0.00226 per share, exercisable for a period of ninety (90) days following the date of termination of Purchaser's continuous service to the Company.

3.2 **Procedure.** The Company may exercise the Repurchase Option by delivering written notice to Purchaser within the ninety (90) day period, specifying the number of unvested Shares to be repurchased. The repurchase shall be effective upon delivery of payment to Purchaser.

---

## ARTICLE 4. RESTRICTIONS ON TRANSFER

4.1 **General Restriction.** Purchaser shall not sell, transfer, assign, pledge, hypothecate, or otherwise dispose of any of the Shares, except as expressly permitted by this Agreement, the Company's Bylaws, and the Co-Founder Agreement v3 (including its right of first refusal, drag-along, and tag-along provisions).

4.2 **Securities Law Compliance.** Purchaser acknowledges that the Shares have not been registered under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the "**Securities Act**"), or any state securities laws, and may not be sold or transferred except pursuant to an effective registration statement under the Securities Act and applicable state securities laws, or pursuant to an exemption therefrom.

4.3 **Legend.** Each certificate representing the Shares shall bear the following legend:

> THE SHARES REPRESENTED HEREBY HAVE NOT BEEN REGISTERED UNDER THE SECURITIES ACT OF 1933 AND MAY NOT BE SOLD OR TRANSFERRED EXCEPT PURSUANT TO AN EFFECTIVE REGISTRATION STATEMENT OR AN EXEMPTION FROM REGISTRATION. THE SHARES ARE SUBJECT TO A RESTRICTED STOCK PURCHASE AGREEMENT, A COPY OF WHICH IS ON FILE AT THE PRINCIPAL OFFICE OF THE COMPANY.

---

## ARTICLE 5. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES OF PURCHASER

5.1 **Investment Intent.** Purchaser is acquiring the Shares for Purchaser's own account, for investment purposes, and not with a view to or for sale in connection with any distribution thereof.

5.2 **Sophistication.** Purchaser has sufficient knowledge and experience in financial and business matters to evaluate the merits and risks of the investment in the Shares, and is able to bear the economic risk of the investment.

5.3 **Access to Information.** Purchaser has had the opportunity to ask questions of and receive answers from the Company concerning the terms of this Agreement, the Shares, and the business of the Company.

5.4 **No Reliance.** Purchaser is not relying on any representation or warranty of the Company other than those expressly set forth in this Agreement.

---

## ARTICLE 6. SECTION 83(b) ELECTION

6.1 **Acknowledgment.** Purchaser acknowledges that, under Section 83 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended (the "**Code**"), the excess (if any) of the fair market value of the Shares on the date such Shares vest over the amount paid therefor will be taxable to Purchaser as ordinary compensation income in the year of vesting.

6.2 **Election.** Purchaser may elect, pursuant to Section 83(b) of the Code, to be taxed currently on the excess (if any) of the fair market value of the Shares on the Effective Date over the Purchase Price (which excess is currently expected to be zero). To make such election, Purchaser must file the election with the Internal Revenue Service within thirty (30) days of the Effective Date.

6.3 **Purchaser's Responsibility.** PURCHASER ACKNOWLEDGES THAT IT IS PURCHASER'S SOLE RESPONSIBILITY, AND NOT THE COMPANY'S, TO TIMELY FILE THE 83(b) ELECTION. THE COMPANY HAS PROVIDED NO TAX ADVICE, AND PURCHASER HAS BEEN ADVISED TO CONSULT WITH PURCHASER'S OWN TAX ADVISOR.

6.4 **Furnishing Copy to Company.** Purchaser shall furnish a copy of any 83(b) election filed by Purchaser to the Company within five (5) business days of mailing the election to the Internal Revenue Service.

---

## ARTICLE 7. MISCELLANEOUS

7.1 **Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflicts of law principles.

7.2 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, together with the Co-Founder Agreement v3, the CIIAA, and the Stock Certificate, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof.

7.3 **Amendment.** This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by both parties.

7.4 **Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, including by electronic signature, each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.

7.5 **Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

7.6 **Successors and Assigns.** This Agreement shall be binding upon and inure to the benefit of the parties and their respective heirs, executors, administrators, successors, and permitted assigns.

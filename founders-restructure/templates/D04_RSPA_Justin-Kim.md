# PHEEEW, INC.
## RESTRICTED STOCK PURCHASE AGREEMENT — JUSTIN KIM

This Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement (this "**Agreement**") is made and entered into as of **[DATE]** (the "**Effective Date**"), by and between **Pheeew, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation (the "**Company**"), and **Justin Kim** ("**Stockholder**").

---

## 1. Sale of Stock

The Company hereby sells to the Stockholder, and the Stockholder hereby purchases from the Company, **three million seventeen thousand one hundred forty-three (3,017,143) shares** of the Company's Common Stock (the "**Shares**") at a purchase price of **$0.00226** per share, for an aggregate purchase price of **$6,818.74** (the "**Purchase Price**").

## 2. Payment

The Stockholder shall pay the Purchase Price by wire transfer to the Company's Mercury operating account, on or before five (5) business days after the Effective Date. Stockholder will additionally contribute **Twenty-Three Thousand One Hundred Eighty-One Dollars and Twenty-Six Cents ($23,181.26)** as Additional Paid-in Capital, for an aggregate capital contribution of **$30,000.00**. The full $30,000.00 shall be wired in a single transaction.

## 3. Vesting Schedule

The Shares shall vest as follows:

(a) **Seven hundred fifty-four thousand two hundred eighty-six (754,286) shares (25%)** shall vest on the one-year anniversary of the Effective Date (the "**Cliff Date**");

(b) Beginning on the first day of the calendar month following the Cliff Date, an additional 1/48th of the total Shares (approximately **62,857.14 shares per month**, prorated) shall vest each month for the next thirty-six (36) months, until 100% of the Shares are vested on the four-year anniversary of the Effective Date.

## 4. Repurchase Option

Upon termination of Stockholder's continuous service to the Company for any reason, the Company shall have the right (but not the obligation) to repurchase all unvested Shares at the original Purchase Price of $0.00226 per share, for a period of ninety (90) days following the date of termination.

## 5. Right of First Refusal

Stockholder grants the Company a right of first refusal on any proposed transfer of vested Shares, on terms equal to the proposed third-party transfer.

## 6. 83(b) Election

Stockholder acknowledges that timely filing of an Internal Revenue Code Section 83(b) election within thirty (30) days of the Effective Date is solely Stockholder's responsibility, and Stockholder agrees to provide a copy of the filed election to the Company within five (5) business days of mailing.

## 7. Acknowledgements

Stockholder acknowledges receipt of and has reviewed the Company's current Certificate of Incorporation (as amended), Bylaws, and the Amended and Restated Co-Founder Agreement (v3), and agrees to be bound by all applicable terms thereof.

## 8. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware.

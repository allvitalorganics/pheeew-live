# PHEEEW, INC.
## RESTRICTED STOCK PURCHASE AGREEMENT (REFRESH) — SEUNGJOON (ALEX) KIM

This Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement (this "**Agreement**") is made and entered into as of **[DATE]** (the "**Effective Date**"), by and between **Pheeew, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation (the "**Company**"), and **Seungjoon (Alex) Kim** ("**Stockholder**").

This Agreement is a **refresh grant** issued in connection with the admission of Justin Kim as a fourth co-founder. It is supplemental to, and does not modify, the Stockholder's original Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement dated April 13, 2026 covering 3,150,000 shares of Common Stock.

---

## 1. Sale of Stock

The Company hereby sells to the Stockholder, and the Stockholder hereby purchases from the Company, **one hundred forty-one thousand four hundred twenty-eight (141,428) shares** of the Company's Common Stock (the "**Refresh Shares**") at a purchase price of **$0.00226** per share, for an aggregate purchase price of **$319.63** (the "**Refresh Purchase Price**").

## 2. Payment

The Stockholder shall pay the Refresh Purchase Price by personal check or wire transfer to the Company's Mercury operating account on or before five (5) business days after the Effective Date.

## 3. Vesting Schedule

The Refresh Shares shall vest as follows:

(a) **Thirty-five thousand three hundred fifty-seven (35,357) shares (25%)** shall vest on the one-year anniversary of the Effective Date (the "**Refresh Cliff Date**");

(b) Beginning on the first day of the calendar month following the Refresh Cliff Date, an additional 1/48th of the total Refresh Shares (approximately **2,946.42 shares per month**, prorated) shall vest each month for the next thirty-six (36) months, until 100% of the Refresh Shares are vested on the four-year anniversary of the Effective Date.

The Refresh Shares vesting schedule is **separate from and additional to** the Stockholder's original 3,150,000 shares (which retain their original April 13, 2026 vesting commencement date and April 13, 2027 cliff).

## 4. Repurchase Option

Upon termination of Stockholder's continuous service to the Company for any reason, the Company shall have the right (but not the obligation) to repurchase all unvested Refresh Shares at the original Refresh Purchase Price of $0.00226 per share, for a period of ninety (90) days following the date of termination.

## 5. Right of First Refusal

Stockholder grants the Company a right of first refusal on any proposed transfer of vested Refresh Shares, on terms equal to the proposed third-party transfer. This right is in addition to the right of first refusal applicable to the Stockholder's original 3,150,000 shares.

## 6. 83(b) Election

Stockholder acknowledges that timely filing of an Internal Revenue Code Section 83(b) election with respect to the Refresh Shares within thirty (30) days of the Effective Date is solely Stockholder's responsibility, and Stockholder agrees to provide a copy of the filed election to the Company within five (5) business days of mailing.

## 7. Self-Transaction Acknowledgment

The parties acknowledge that the Stockholder also serves as the Chief Executive Officer of the Company and that this Agreement is being executed by the Stockholder in both capacities. The Stockholders' Written Consent dated **[DATE]** has ratified this self-transaction in accordance with Section 144 of the General Corporation Law of the State of Delaware.

## 8. Acknowledgements

Stockholder acknowledges receipt of and has reviewed the Company's current Certificate of Incorporation (as amended), Bylaws, and the Amended and Restated Co-Founder Agreement (v3), and agrees to be bound by all applicable terms thereof.

## 9. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware.

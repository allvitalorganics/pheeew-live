# WRITTEN CONSENT OF STOCKHOLDERS
## PHEEEW, INC.

The undersigned, being the holders of all of the outstanding shares of Common Stock of Pheeew, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the "**Company**"), in lieu of holding a meeting, hereby take the following actions and adopt the following resolutions effective as of **[DATE]**:

---

## 1. Approval of Amendment to Certificate of Incorporation

**RESOLVED**, that the Certificate of Amendment to the Certificate of Incorporation, in the form attached hereto as **Exhibit A**, increasing the authorized Common Stock from ten million (10,000,000) shares to fourteen million (14,000,000) shares, par value $0.0001 per share, is hereby approved.

---

## 2. Approval of Share Issuances

**RESOLVED**, that the issuance of three million seventeen thousand one hundred forty-three (3,017,143) shares of Common Stock to Justin Kim, one hundred forty-one thousand four hundred twenty-eight (141,428) shares of Common Stock to Seungjoon (Alex) Kim, and one hundred eighty-four thousand two hundred eighty-six (184,286) shares of Common Stock to Jason Ko, each at $0.00226 per share, are hereby approved.

---

## 3. Approval of Amended Co-Founder Agreement

**RESOLVED**, that the Amended and Restated Co-Founder Agreement (v3), substantially in the form attached hereto as **Exhibit B**, is hereby approved.

---

## 4. Ratification of Self-Transactions

**RESOLVED**, that the issuance of additional shares of Common Stock to Seungjoon (Alex) Kim under his Refresh Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement, including any acts of Seungjoon (Alex) Kim acting in his capacity both as Stockholder and as Chief Executive Officer of the Company in connection therewith, is hereby ratified and approved as a transaction in the Company's interest, having been disclosed to and approved by all stockholders of the Company.

---

**IN WITNESS WHEREOF**, the undersigned have executed this Written Consent of Stockholders as of the date first written above.

Pause. Skip. Cancel anytime.

Effective May 15, 2026 · Pheeew, Inc., a Delaware C-Corporation

These Subscription Terms apply to any subscription order placed on pheeew.com.

1. How it works

Pick a pack size (20, 30, 60, or 120 pouches) and a frequency (typically monthly). Your card on file is automatically charged on the renewal date, and the next pack ships within 1–2 business days. You're saving 17–27% off one-time pricing.

2. Pause, skip, cancel

Anytime, from your account dashboard, no phone call required. Pausing keeps your subscription active without shipping. Skipping advances the next shipment by one cycle. Canceling ends the subscription — you can restart anytime.

To avoid being charged for an upcoming shipment, make the change at least 2 days before the renewal date listed in your account.

3. Renewal reminders

We send an email 3 days before each renewal so you can review or change before processing.

4. Price changes

We'll notify you by email at least 30 days in advance of any price increase. You can cancel before the new price takes effect.

5. Refunds on subscription shipments

Subscription shipments fall under our standard Refund Policy — 30 days from delivery for unopened packs. To avoid the refund process entirely, pause or skip before the renewal.

6. Subscription billing

Billing is processed by Recharge (our subscription platform) using the payment method on file. You'll receive a receipt by email after each charge. Failed charges are retried up to three times — after that the subscription is paused until you update your payment.

7. Account access

Manage everything (frequency, pack size, address, payment, pause, cancel) from your customer account. Lost access? Email help@pheeew.com and we'll help.

8. California Auto-Renewal Disclosure

This is an auto-renewal program. By starting a subscription, you authorize Pheeew, Inc. (and our payment processor Recharge) to charge your payment method on file at the recurring frequency you select, at the price disclosed at checkout, until you cancel. You can cancel anytime from your account dashboard with no penalty.

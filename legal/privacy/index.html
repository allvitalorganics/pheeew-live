◆ Privacy

How we handle your data.

Effective May 15, 2026 · Pheeew, Inc., a Delaware C-Corporation

This Privacy Policy explains how Pheeew, Inc. ("Pheeew," "we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit pheeew.com, place an order, sign up for our waitlist, or otherwise interact with us. By using the site or our services, you agree to this Policy.

1. Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Account & order info: name, email, phone, shipping and billing addresses, order history, subscription preferences.
  • Payment info: processed by Shopify Payments and our subscription processor (Recharge). Pheeew does not store full card numbers — only a token that lets us run the charge.
  • Communications: emails you send us, reviews you post, survey responses, support tickets.
  • Marketing opt-ins: waitlist signups, newsletter subscriptions, SMS opt-ins.

Information collected automatically

  • Site usage: IP address, device type, browser, pages viewed, referring URL, timestamps. Collected via cookies and similar technologies (see Cookie Policy).
  • Order analytics: aggregate revenue, conversion, traffic source via Shopify, Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, and TikTok Pixel.

2. How we use information

  • To fulfill orders, process payments, ship product, and manage subscriptions.
  • To send order confirmations, shipping notifications, and customer service replies.
  • To send marketing emails or SMS you've opted into. You can unsubscribe anytime.
  • To improve the site, products, and customer experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations (tax reporting, recall notices, regulatory inquiries).

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share with:

  • Service providers who help us run the business: Shopify (e-commerce), Klaviyo (email/SMS), Recharge (subscriptions), our 3PL (fulfillment), payment processors, and analytics providers.
  • Legal compliance: if required by law, court order, or government request.
  • Business transfers: in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

4. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information. Email hello@pheeew.com with the subject line "Privacy Request" and we'll respond within 30 days. California residents have additional rights under CCPA — see our CCPA Notice.

5. Children

Pheeew is not targeted at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe we have, contact us and we'll delete it.

6. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards (encryption in transit, access controls, vendor security reviews). No system is 100% secure — if we ever suffer a breach affecting your information, we'll notify you in line with applicable state breach-notification laws.

7. International users

Pheeew currently ships only within the United States. If you access the site from outside the US, you understand your information will be transferred to and processed in the US.

8. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be announced via email or a prominent notice on the site. The "Effective" date above tracks the latest version.

9. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints: hello@pheeew.com.

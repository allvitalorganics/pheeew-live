In-person legal info.

Effective May 15, 2026 · Pheeew, Inc., a Delaware C-Corporation

This page documents what to do if you encounter Pheeew in a physical retail or in-person context — at a pop-up, a trade show, a wholesale account, or anywhere outside pheeew.com.

1. Physical product

Every Pheeew pouch carries the required regulatory labeling on the package itself:

  • Product name (Mojave Sunrise)
  • Ingredients list, including all allergens
  • Nutrition Facts panel (FDA-compliant)
  • Net contents (80 mL)
  • Country of origin: "Product of Korea"
  • Distributor line: "Distributed by Pheeew, Inc., Los Angeles, CA"
  • Lot number and best-by date
  • Storage instructions

2. Product safety & quality

Pheeew is manufactured in a FDA-registered facility in South Korea operating under HACCP and GMP standards. Every batch is accompanied by a Certificate of Analysis covering microbial counts, CFU verification, fiber content, pH, and fill volume. CoAs are on file with the importer of record (Pheeew, Inc.) and available to wholesale buyers and regulators on request.

3. Wholesale & retail compliance

Pheeew, Inc. carries general liability and product liability insurance. Certificates of insurance and supplier compliance documentation (FSVP, food facility registration, allergen statements, kosher/halal/vegan certifications as applicable) are available to verified wholesale buyers. Contact sales@pheeew.com with "Wholesale compliance" in the subject.

4. Product recall procedure

In the unlikely event of a recall, Pheeew, Inc. follows the FDA's standard recall protocol:

  1. Notify wholesale partners and direct customers via email and posted notice on pheeew.com.
  2. Stop distribution of affected lots.
  3. Provide return/refund/replacement options at no cost to the customer.
  4. Coordinate with FDA on classification and effectiveness reporting.

5. Adverse event reporting

If you have a Pheeew pouch you believe caused an adverse reaction, please:

  1. Stop consumption.
  2. Save the pouch including the lot number printed on the bottom.
  3. Email help@pheeew.com with "Adverse event" in the subject line, with date of consumption, symptoms, and lot number.
  4. Seek medical care if symptoms warrant.

We investigate every report. Reports are aggregated, anonymized, and reviewed by our QA team alongside the manufacturer.

◆ Accessibility

For everyone.

Effective May 15, 2026 · Pheeew, Inc., a Delaware C-Corporation

Pheeew is committed to making pheeew.com accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We're working to meet Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA on an ongoing basis.

1. What we've done

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy.
  • Sufficient color contrast on text and interactive elements.
  • Keyboard navigation for menus, forms, and the customer account.
  • Alt text on product imagery (in progress as photo assets land).
  • Responsive layouts that work at 200%+ zoom and on mobile screens.
  • Form fields with associated labels and clear error messaging.

2. What's still in progress

We're a small team building this in public. Some pages may still have accessibility gaps — particularly around screen reader testing and video captioning (when product video launches). We treat every accessibility report as a priority.

3. Report an issue

Found something that doesn't work? Email hello@pheeew.com with "Accessibility" in the subject line. Tell us:

  • The page URL you were on
  • The action you were trying to take
  • What happened instead
  • The assistive technology you were using (if any)

We respond within 2 business days and work to fix verified issues in the next site update.

4. Alternative formats

If you need product information in an alternative format, email us and we'll work with you to provide it.

5. Third-party content

Some elements of the site (embedded video, social feeds, payment processor pages) are served by third parties and may not meet our internal accessibility standards. We're advocating with those vendors to improve.

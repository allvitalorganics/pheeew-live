How we use cookies.

Effective May 15, 2026 · Pheeew, Inc., a Delaware C-Corporation

This Cookie Policy explains how Pheeew uses cookies and similar technologies on pheeew.com.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember things like your login state, cart contents, and preferences. Similar technologies include pixels, local storage, and session storage.

2. Cookies we use

Strictly necessary

  • Cart & checkout: remembers items in your cart and processes your order (Shopify).
  • Authentication: keeps you logged into your account.
  • Site preview gate: remembers you've entered the pre-launch passcode for the current session.

Analytics

  • Google Analytics: aggregate site usage (page views, traffic source, device type).
  • Shopify Analytics: aggregate sales and conversion data.

Marketing & advertising

  • Meta Pixel: tracks site activity for Facebook/Instagram ad measurement (only if we run paid ads).
  • TikTok Pixel: tracks site activity for TikTok ad measurement (only if we run paid ads).
  • Klaviyo: identifies returning visitors for personalized email content.

3. Your choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security

Blocking strictly necessary cookies will break site functionality (cart, login). Blocking analytics or marketing cookies does not affect site functionality.

California residents can opt out of cross-context behavioral advertising via the CCPA process.

4. Do Not Track

We do not respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals because there is no universally accepted standard. We do honor verified Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals from California residents.

5. Changes

If we materially change our cookie practices, we will update this page and post a notice on the site.

Your rights under CCPA & CPRA.

Effective May 15, 2026 · Pheeew, Inc., a Delaware C-Corporation

This Notice supplements our Privacy Policy and applies to California residents under the California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018 ("CCPA") as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act ("CPRA").

1. Personal information we collect

In the past 12 months we have collected the following categories of personal information about California residents:

  • Identifiers: name, email, postal address, phone number, account credentials, IP address.
  • Commercial information: order history, subscription preferences, payment tokens (not full card numbers).
  • Internet activity: site usage, page views, referring URL, device and browser data.
  • Geolocation: approximate location derived from IP address (for shipping rate calculation).
  • Inferences: preferences based on order history and browsing behavior, for personalized email content.

2. Sources of information

Directly from you (orders, signups, support requests), automatically from your device (cookies, analytics), and from service providers (payment processors, subscription platform).

3. Why we collect it

To fulfill orders, manage subscriptions, ship product, send transactional and marketing communications you've opted into, prevent fraud, improve the site, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell personal information.

4. Sharing

We share with service providers (Shopify, Klaviyo, Recharge, our 3PL, payment processors, analytics) under written agreements that limit their use to providing services to Pheeew.

5. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we've collected, used, disclosed, or sold about you.
  • Delete personal information we hold about you, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Opt-out of sale or sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising. (Pheeew does not sell personal information.)
  • Limit use of sensitive personal information.
  • Not be retaliated against for exercising any of these rights.

6. How to exercise rights

Email hello@pheeew.com with the subject "CCPA Request" and the specific right you're exercising. We'll verify your identity (typically by confirming the email and order details on file) and respond within 45 days. You may use an authorized agent if you provide written permission.

7. Notice of financial incentives

Our Subscribe & Save program offers price discounts to customers who provide personal information to set up a recurring subscription. The value of the information we collect (your email, address, and order frequency) is reasonably related to the discount offered. You can opt out and unsubscribe at any time without penalty.

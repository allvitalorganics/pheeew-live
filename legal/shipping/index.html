◆ Shipping

Free shipping over \$50.

Effective May 15, 2026 · Pheeew, Inc., a Delaware C-Corporation

1. Where we ship

Continental United States, Alaska, and Hawaii. We do not ship internationally in Year 1. APO/FPO/DPO addresses are not currently supported but may be added based on demand.

2. Shipping rates

  • Orders $50+: free standard shipping.
  • Orders under $50: flat $7.95 standard shipping.
  • Alaska & Hawaii: flat $14.95 regardless of order value.
  • Expedited shipping: available at checkout for an additional fee.

3. Processing & delivery time

Orders placed before 12:00 PM PT on a business day ship the same day. Orders placed after 12:00 PM PT or on weekends ship the next business day. Once shipped, standard delivery is 3–7 business days. Expedited delivery is 1–3 business days.

4. Subscription shipments

Subscription orders ship on a regular cadence based on your pack size and frequency. You can preview, edit, pause, or skip upcoming shipments from your account before the renewal processes.

5. Tracking

You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking link as soon as your order is on the truck.

6. Lost or stolen packages

If your tracking shows delivered but you haven't received it, check with neighbors and your local carrier first. Then email hello@pheeew.com with your order number — we'll work with you on a replacement.

7. Address changes

We can change your shipping address before the order ships. Once it's on the carrier, we can't reroute it. Update your default subscription address from your account at any time.

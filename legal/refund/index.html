◆ Refunds & returns

30 days. Unopened pack. Full refund.

Effective May 15, 2026 · Pheeew, Inc., a Delaware C-Corporation

We want you to feel good about your purchase. This Refund Policy covers returns, refunds, and exchanges for orders placed on pheeew.com.

1. The promise

Unopened packs can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund of the product price. Shipping costs are not refunded. The return is processed once we receive the returned pack at our warehouse.

2. If you've started the pack

If you've opened the pack and Pheeew isn't right for you, contact us at hello@pheeew.com within 30 days of delivery. We'll work with you on a one-time goodwill refund or replacement. We treat this as a conversation, not a confrontation — but we reserve the right to limit goodwill refunds to one per household.

3. Damaged or wrong items

If your order arrives damaged or you received the wrong item, email hello@pheeew.com within 7 days of delivery with a photo. We'll replace or refund at no cost to you — no return required.

4. Subscription orders

Subscription orders fall under the same 30-day window per delivery. To avoid being billed for a shipment you don't want, pause or cancel from your account before the renewal date listed in your subscription dashboard.

5. How to start a return

  1. Email hello@pheeew.com with your order number and the reason.
  2. We'll send a return label and instructions within 1 business day.
  3. Ship the unopened pack back. Refund is processed within 5 business days of receipt.

6. Non-returnable items

Gift cards, free promotional samples, and items marked "final sale" are non-returnable.

7. Questions

hello@pheeew.com — we reply inside one business day.

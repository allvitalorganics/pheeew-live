The product

What is it?

An 80mL synbiotic puree pouch. 10g of fiber. 1 billion CFU of a probiotic. Real fruit. One a day.

Why a pouch?

Capsules need an empty stomach. Powders need a blender. Bottles need a fridge. The pouch needs none of that. It survives the habit.

Supplement or food?

Food. The probiotic is GRAS-affirmed, so no "this has not been evaluated" disclaimer. We don't claim it cures anything.

How does it taste?

Tropical fruit, mango, banana. Sweet from the fruit — no added sugar. Smoothie at room temp. Slushy cold. Popsicle frozen.

When are the other flavors?

Berry and Ginger & Pear are on the roadmap. Order pace depends on what people actually ask for. Vote on the contact page.

What's inside

What does the prebiotic fiber do for me?

It feeds the good bacteria already living in your gut so they can do their job — keep you regular, less bloated, more comfortable after meals. 10g per pouch is about a third of what the FDA recommends per day. Most adults run a 40–60% shortfall, so this is the daily fiber most people are missing.

What does the probiotic do for me?

It's live bacteria you actually need. Most probiotics die in your stomach before they reach where they're supposed to work. Ours is a spore-form strain that survives the trip — and 30+ clinical studies on this strain show improvements in regularity, bloat, transit time, and immune markers.

What's the full ingredient list?

Four lines. Real fruit puree (mango, banana, tropical fruit). Prebiotic fiber blend (10g). Probiotic strain (1B CFU). Lemon juice. That's it.

Added sugar?

None. Sweetness comes from the fruit. No cane sugar, no agave, no high-fructose anything. No sucralose, no stevia, no monk fruit either — we don't use sweeteners of any kind.

Natural flavor? Coloring? Preservatives?

None of those either. No flavoring of any kind. No coloring. No preservatives. The shelf life comes from the hot-fill process and a touch of lemon juice — that's the whole story.

Allergens?

None of the top 9. Vegan. Gluten-free. Non-GMO. Allergen-free production line.

How to use

When do I drink it?

Whenever. Most people pick mornings because consistent timing makes the habit stick faster.

When do I feel something?

Week 1: gut wakes up. Week 2–3: rhythm. Week 4–6: compound effect. Week 8: it's routine.

Does it need a fridge?

No. Shelf-stable 18 months at room temp. Cold tastes better. The cap reseals if you don't finish a pouch.

How does the pouch open?

Child-safe screw cap. Twist to open, twist to reseal. No tearing, no spilling, no scissors required.

With other supplements or meds?

No known interactions at the doses we use. We're not your doctor — if you're on a specific protocol, ask one.
